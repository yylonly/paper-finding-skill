## Notes

- **Verification vs Validation:** In requirements engineering, "validation" ensures requirements match stakeholder needs; "verification" ensures implementation meets specified requirements. Both matter.
- **DBLP venue strings** use abbreviations: "ICSE", "FSE", "AAAI", "NeurIPS", etc. Journals appear as venue abbreviations like "TSE", "TOSEM", "AI", etc.
- **Workshops** (REW, MoDRE, etc.) are typically CCF-C or unranked — exclude unless specifically needed.
- **Page counts:** ICSE NIER/Companion papers are typically 4-6 pages (short papers). Full research papers are 10-12 pages.
- Cross-reference page counts via ACM Digital Library or IEEE Xplore for accuracy.
